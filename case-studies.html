Roofing Company: Voice-Enabled Field App

The Challenge

A commercial roofing company was losing hours of productivity daily as crews had to remove gloves in cold weather to manually enter inspection data on tablets. Information was incomplete and often lost entirely.

The Solution

We built a voice-enabled mobile app that allows workers to capture inspection details hands-free. The app works offline and automatically syncs with Salesforce when internet connection is available. AI transcription ensures accuracy and auto-populates fields.

Key Features

  • Voice-to-text data capture
  • Offline-first architecture
  • Photo capture with auto-tagging
  • Real-time Salesforce sync
  • AI-powered quality checks

The Results

  • Data entry time reduced from 15 minutes to 2 minutes per inspection
  • 100% data capture rate (up from 60%)
  • Crews can complete 3 more inspections per day
  • Customer satisfaction scores increased 40%
